Top five reasons why you should consider joining AASTU

  • Addis Ababa Science and Technology University is recognized in Africa, you will have the opportunity to get an internationally recognized qualification.
  • At the Addis Ababa Science and Technology University, you will have the opportunity to hone your leadership skills through engagement in societies, clubs, residence committees
  • It will expose you to some of the best researchers and teachers in the world.
  • Addis Ababa Science and Technology University s programs have been carefully designed to ensure that each student is well prepared for the Work environment of the 21st century.
  • AASTU graduates have jobs before they qualify and almost all have jobs within a few months after graduating, unless they choose for a specific reason not to enter formal enmployment.
